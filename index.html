About Me

I’m your fairly regular guy. A man’s man. Pickle jars will sometimes open when I just look at them. Can’t come down the condiment isle without causing a terrible mess.

I’ve always thought of myself as good-natured person. I enjoy music, science, politics, sci-fi, math, puns, crime mysteries, food and beer. Like most people, I enjoy traveling. I’ll ski in the winter and do festivals in the summer.

These days, I'd say I'm mostly going a little harder with work than play.
