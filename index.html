About Me

I’m your fairly regular guy. A man’s man. Pickle jars open when I look at them. Can’t come down the condiment isle without causing a terrible mess. I’ve always thought of myself as good-natured person. I enjoy music, science, politics, sci-fi, math, puns, crime mysteries, food and beer. Like most people, I enjoy traveling. I’ll ski in the winter and do festivals in the summer. I'd say I balance a pretty good mixture of work and play. Both of which, I go pretty hard.
